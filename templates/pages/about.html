Who We Are

General Food Industry Co., Ltd. (GFI) is a Bangkok-based company specialising in the import, export, and distribution of food additives and ingredients. We serve food and beverage manufacturers, processors, and producers throughout Thailand and across Southeast Asia.

Founded on a commitment to quality and traceability, GFI works directly with certified global suppliers to ensure every product we handle meets the highest food safety standards. Our team combines deep product knowledge with strong logistics capabilities to deliver reliable supply chain solutions.

We believe in building lasting partnerships — understanding our customers' formulation needs and supporting them with consistent supply, technical guidance, and responsive service.
